About

I'm a Branco Weiss fellow at Stanford in Polly Fordyce's lab. I create microfluidic devices, large-scale datasets, and machine learning algorithms to understand molecular biology.

I did my PhD in computer science at UC Berkeley with Michael I. Jordan, where I studied machine learning systems from a data-centric perspective.
